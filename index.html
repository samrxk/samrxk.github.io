Nestlé Bakers' Corner

ideation

Challenge: find a white space for Nestlé Bakers' Corner to exist on Instagram, amongst all of the #foodporn.

Working: I held a creative workshop with my team and the Bakers' Corner brand team and concepted multiple ideas,
landing on the current 'long table' execution. I led the project and worked closely with the brand manager, photographer, and food stylist in pre-production.

Result: grew the channel following by over 1.5k in 5 months.

Because the execution can be stopped and started anywhere, it has longevity to run forever, incorporating both brand and influencer partnerships in the future.

Check it out on Instagram @bakerscorner.
